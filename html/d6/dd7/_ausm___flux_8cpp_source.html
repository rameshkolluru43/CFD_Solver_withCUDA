9 *
10 * AUSM is a flux vector splitting scheme that splits the flux into convective
11 * and pressure parts. It's particularly effective for low Mach number flows
12 * and provides good shock-capturing capabilities.
13 *
14 * Mathematical Framework:
15 * F = F_c + F_p
16 * where F_c is the convective flux and F_p is the pressure flux
17 *
18 * This implementation computes the AUSM flux for all faces of the given cell
