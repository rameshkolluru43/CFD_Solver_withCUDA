\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}Quantum Fourier Transform Gate.

The Quantum Fourier Transform (QFT) on :math:`n` qubits is the operation

.. math::

    |j\rangle \mapsto \frac{1}{2^{n/2}} \sum_{k=0}^{2^n - 1} e^{2\pi ijk / 2^n} |k\rangle\end{DoxyVerb}
