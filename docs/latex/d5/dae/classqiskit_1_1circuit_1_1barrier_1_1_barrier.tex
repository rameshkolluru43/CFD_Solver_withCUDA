\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}A directive for circuit compilation to separate pieces of a circuit so that any optimizations
or re-writes are constrained to only act between barriers.

This will also appear in visualizations as a visual marker.
\end{DoxyVerb}
